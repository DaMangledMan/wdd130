Growing up, cooking with my mom wasn't just about preparing meals; it was a sacred ritual. Her gentle guidance, her infectious laughter, and her unwavering patience turned the kitchen into a haven of love and learning. We'd spend hours chopping vegetables, stirring sauces, and baking treats, sharing stories and dreams as we worked. These shared experiences shaped my palate, my cooking skills, and my understanding of food as a language of love. Now, as I navigate the challenges of college life, I find solace in the memories of those cozy kitchen moments. They remind me of the warmth of family, the comfort of home, and the simple joy of sharing a meal with loved ones.
